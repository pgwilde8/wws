Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about builds, third-party costs, support, data, and how to get started.

Third-party services (OpenAI, Twilio, Stripe, Cloudflare)

  • What services do you integrate?
    Commonly OpenAI (AI features), Twilio (voice/SMS), Stripe (payments), Cloudflare (DNS/security).
  • What rates should we expect from OpenAI?
    Usage-based by model and tokens. Light chat often lands in the tens of dollars monthly; heavier automation costs more. Exact rates depend on the model and volume.
  • What rates should we expect from Twilio?
    Per-number monthly fee plus per-minute voice and per-SMS charges. US numbers are low monthly cost; usage scales with call/SMS volume.
  • Do you add a markup to third-party fees?
    No. You pay providers directly; we configure and integrate.
  • Can we cap or monitor usage?
    Yes. We can help set sensible defaults and usage alerts.

Build process & scope

  • What’s included?
    Your chosen package: site, funnels, AI chat/voice wiring, CRM/email/SMS workflows as scoped.
  • What’s not included?
    Ongoing design/content changes, copywriting, or marketing ops unless added as an ongoing plan.
  • How long does a build take?
    Starter ~2–3 weeks; Growth ~3–5 weeks; custom/Scale depends on complexity.
  • Do I own what you build?
    Yes. You own the code, data, and configuration delivered.

Support & changes

  • What support is included?
    Keeping the system running: bug fixes, maintaining automations/integrations, ensuring it operates as designed.
  • What’s not included in base support?
    Frequent design tweaks, ongoing copy/content production, marketing ops, or repetitive asset swaps.
  • Do you offer a design/content add-on?
    Yes, we can scope a monthly plan for creative updates and asset changes—book a call to discuss.

Data, security, ownership

  • Who controls accounts and credentials?
    You do. We set up and hand off; billing stays in your accounts.
  • Is my data private?
    We follow provider guidelines and best practices; your data stays in your accounts/services.
  • Can we get usage/export access?
    Yes. You retain access to dashboards and data in your connected services.

Getting started

  • What do you need from me?
    Business basics (name, industry, audience), brand preferences, copy/offer details, domain/DNS access, and your API keys (Stripe, Twilio, OpenAI, etc.).
  • How do I choose the right package?
    Take our 6-question quiz or book a call; we’ll recommend Starter, Growth, or a custom scope based on complexity and volume.
  • Can we explore custom work?
    Yes—book a call for a custom scope when you need deeper engineering, data/AI, or complex workflows.

Twilio example rates (U.S. baseline)

These are illustrative only; actual pricing varies by country, number type, carrier fees, and volume. Always confirm current rates in your Twilio account.

  • SMS: ~\$0.0079 per message (inbound/outbound).
  • MMS: ~\$0.010 inbound / \$0.020 outbound per message.
  • Voice: ~\$0.0085/min inbound; ~\$0.014/min outbound (toll-free inbound can be higher).
  • Number rental: ~\$1–\$2/mo per U.S. number.

Example: 10,000 outbound SMS ≈ 10,000 × \$0.0079 = \$79 (plus any carrier pass-through fees).

International rates differ by country; check Twilio’s pricing tables or API for precise numbers.

OpenAI example rates (token-based, illustrative)

OpenAI bills per input/output tokens. Costs below are rough examples; always confirm current pricing in your account.

Model Input (per 1M tokens) Output (per 1M tokens) Notes
GPT-5.2 ~$1.75 ~$14.00 High-end agentic tasks
GPT-4.1 ~$2.00 ~$8.00 Strong chat quality
GPT-4o / similar ~$5.00 ~$10.00 Multimodal/advanced
Cheaper “mini” models ~$0.15–$0.60 Typically 4–16× higher than input Best for high-volume/simple flows

Example (medium chatbot): 30k messages/month × ~600 tokens each = 18M tokens total.

  • GPT-4.1: ~($2.00 + $8.00) × 18 = ~$180/mo (illustrative)
  • GPT-5.2: ~($1.75 + $14.00) × 18 = ~$283.50/mo (illustrative)
  • Cheaper mini model: Roughly ~$13–$20/mo (illustrative)

Actual costs depend on model choice, token lengths, and optimization (summaries, shorter prompts). Always verify current pricing in your OpenAI account.
