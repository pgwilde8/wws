Common Questions

What's included in support?

Every build includes support to keep your machine running: fixing bugs, maintaining automations and integrations, and ensuring your system continues operating as designed. Support means keeping the engine healthy, not unlimited design tweaks or content changes.

Can I update content myself?

Yes! We build your site so you can handle basic content updates yourself. You'll have an admin area where you can update content without opening a ticket or waiting on us. You stay in control of your content, we stay focused on keeping the machine healthy.

Do I own what you build?

Absolutely. You own what we build. This isn't a rented template. You're buying infrastructure — a machine you control. The code, the database, the system — it's all yours.

Which plan is right for me?

Starter if what you want already exists as a product you could buy today.
Growth if you have traction and need multiple systems working together.
Scale if what you want doesn't exist yet and requires custom engineering, data, or AI.
